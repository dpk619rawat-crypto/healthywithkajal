My Story

Passionate about healing through food

Hi, I'm Kajal Shrivastava — a certified dietician, nutritionist, and wellness coach with 8+ years of experience. My journey began when I saw close family members struggle with diabetes and PCOS, and realised that most medical advice was either generic or disconnected from Indian food culture.

I created Kajal Nutrition Studio to bridge that gap — combining the precision of clinical nutrition with the warmth of Indian cooking traditions. I believe that healing should never mean giving up the foods you love.
